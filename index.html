Blog Shovel: Dig Deep Into Blogspace With URL Patterns

Blog Shovel uses URL patterns to help you dig deep into the Web's blog and news space and surface old results. Specify a query, the year you want your search to start and finish, how you want to restrict the content of your results (if at all), and how you want to restrict your search by top-level domain (if at all.)

Blog Shovel builds a Google query based on your specifications and opens it in a new window.
